About Us

Our Team


Nidhi Nair

Co-Founder
[Board Chairman And Director]

Before founding Drop of Life in 2021, Nidhi was a water deliquent that took two hour showers. She did not care about the enviornment at all and was a proud litterer. Her house's water bill got so bad that her parents shipped her away to nature boot camp. She came back a new woman. Ever since, she has been on a mission to bring clean water everywhere. Along with two of her classmates at High Technology High School, she decided to create a nonprofit. In her free time, Nidhi likes to watch Charles Leclerc race and horror movies. She also likes to call her best friend Rebecca for three hours at a time. For some godforsaken reason, she loves to chew bubble gum (mint is superior).


Rebecca Wang

Co-Founder
[Board Secretary And Director]

Rebecca has always had a passion for the enviornment. She has also known Nidhi for practically her whole life. She has first hand witnessed her abuse of water. One day, she snapped and couldn't stand by and do nothing. Rebecca convinced Nidhi's parents to ship her off to nature boot camp. After she returned, they were finally in agreement about their passion for the environment. They decided to found a charity to bring clean water to those who didn't have any. She is currently a junior at High Technology High School. In her free time, Rebecca loves to bike, swim, play soccer, play cards, and hang out with her friends. She calls Nidhi for four hours at a time and roots against Charles Leclerc.


Emme Cai

Co-Founder
[Board Treasurer And Director]

Emme has always loved environmental engineering, and attends the #2 NJ STEM school, High Technology High School. Once hearing about the atrocities Nidhi was commiting, she felt the need to create a charity to sort of balance out all the harm Nidhi was doing. Once Nidhi returned from boot camp with a sudden change of heart, Emme had a great idea: What would be better than a reformed water delinquent cofounding a water charity? In her free time, she loves to draw, do fluid dynamics practice problems, and test samples of water in the lab.

Join Us To Make A Life-Changing Impact


Together, we can change lives by bringing clean water, handwashing facilities, sanitation and hygiene education to children who lack basic access to clean water. With your help, we can give communities hope, reduce cases of illness, and give children the chance to focus on their education.
