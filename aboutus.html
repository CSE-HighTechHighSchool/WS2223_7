About Us

Our Team

Nidhi Nair

Co-Founder
[Board Chairman And Director]

Before founding Drop of Life in 2021, Nidhi was a water deliquent that took 2 hour showers. She did not care about the enviornment at all and was a proud litterer. Her house's water bill got so bad that her parents shipped her away to nature boot camp. She came back a new woman. Ever since, she has been on a mission to bring clean water everywhere. Along with two of her classmates at High Technology High School, she decided to create a nonprofit.

Rebecca Wang

Co-Founder
[Board Secretary And Director]

Rebecca has always had a passion for the enviornment. She has also known Nidhi for practically her whole life. She has first hand witnessed her abuse of water. One day, she snapped and couldn't stand by and do nothing. Rebecca convinced Nidhi's parents to ship her off to nature boot camp. After she returned, they were finally in agreement about their passion for the environment. They decided to found a charity to bring clean water to those who didn't have any.
