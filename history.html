Our Mission/History

Our Mission


To improve the health and overall lives of people in the world’s most impoverished communities through providing access to safe drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene education.


Our Story


Dec '20

Initial Brainstorming

Our founders were three young girls who had a passion for engineering and the desire to make a lasting impact in the world. One glaring issue then an now is a shortage of clean water around the world. They decided to found a charity to bring what the believed was a basic necesity to less fortunate people around the world.


Jun '21

Establishment

Drop of Life was officially established and approved on June 20, 2021. We got to work immediately, starting our first projects in the United States and Mexico. Our sponsors helped fund water, sanitation, and hygiene programs for rural communities.

Apr '22

Recognition

In April, we were honored to receive the 2022 Best New Charity Award from the Charity Times Awards and the Third Sector Awards. This helped us gain recognition, and expand our projects to Latin America, Sub-Saharan Africa, and Asia.


Pres.

Our Growing Impact

From then, we've grown into a sizeable community of volunteers and have taken a large step in providing clean water to all. We operate in multiple countries and have thousands of volunteers. Ever since 2020, we've been working towards one goal, and one goal only: ending the global water crisis. We hope that others around the world understand our cause and rally against the global water shortage with us.
