History of Our Charity

Our Story


Our founders were three young girls who had a passion for engineering and the desire to make a lasting impact in the world. One glaring issue then an now is a shortage of clean water around the world.

From then, we've grown into a sizeable community of volunteers and have taken a large step in providing clean water to all. We hope that others around the world understand our cause and rally against the global water shortage with us.
