The Drop of Life Foundation

We are a non-profit organization focused on addressing the global water crisis. There are communities that do not have access to clean water, a resource universally acknowledged as a necessity. We aim to bridge this gap by bringing access to water sources and filtration to those who most need it.

Learn More About Who We Are

The impact of our programs stretches from Asia-Pacific region to Latin America and Africa. Together we can ensure that everyone has access to the water they need to live.

Since 2022, Drop of Life has led projects in 23 countries, helping communities gain access to clean, safe drinking water and hygiene education.
